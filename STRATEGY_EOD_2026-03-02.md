# EOD Strategy Sprint — 2026-03-02

## Goal
Ship one profitable online identity + MVP funnel today.

## Constraints
- Low/zero-cost tooling first
- Ethical/legal distribution only
- Measurable ROI path

## Option 1 — AI Automation Studio Brand (Website + LinkedIn/X)
### Concept
Create a micro-agency identity helping creators/solo businesses automate lead-gen and content operations.

### Monetization
- Productized service tiers ($299 setup, $99/mo maintenance)
- Upsell: custom workflow builds ($500–$1,500)

### Best Practices
- One-page website with clear niche, outcomes, proof, CTA
- Book-call + async audit form
- Niche-first messaging (e.g., coaches, local service businesses)

### 7-day Validation
- 30 outbound DMs + 10 targeted posts + 1 free teardown offer
- KPI: 3 discovery calls, 1 paying client

### Pros
- Fast to revenue
- High-ticket potential

### Risks
- Requires sales outreach discipline

---

## Option 2 — Faceless Trend Media Brand (Social-first)
### Concept
Launch a faceless short-form account around AI productivity/income systems, funneling to a newsletter + affiliate stack.

### Monetization
- Affiliate tools
- Sponsorships (after traction)
- Paid templates/notion packs

### Best Practices
- 3 content pillars (tutorial, case study, myth-busting)
- CTA to lead magnet + email capture
- Repurpose each script across TikTok/Reels/Shorts/X threads

### 14-day Validation
- Publish 2 clips/day for 14 days
- KPI: 1,000 followers total, 200 email subs, first affiliate conversion

### Pros
- Compounding audience asset
- Low startup cost

### Risks
- Volume consistency required

---

## Option 3 — Niche Micro-SaaS Landing + Waitlist (App/Website)
### Concept
Build a narrow tool (e.g., “AI Content Repurposer for Realtors”) with waitlist + pre-sell before full build.

### Monetization
- Pre-sell lifetime deals ($49–$149)
- Monthly subscription post-MVP ($19–$49/mo)

### Best Practices
- Problem-specific landing page
- Demo GIF + use-case proof
- Stripe pre-order or waitlist intent capture

### 7-day Validation
- 3 niche communities + 1 founder story thread + 20 direct outreaches
- KPI: 20 waitlist signups + 3 preorders

### Pros
- Scalable asset
- Potential recurring MRR

### Risks
- Product build complexity if demand unclear

---
